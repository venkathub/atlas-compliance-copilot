# BSA/AML Regulatory Reference (Public Summary)

This non-confidential reference summarizes the principal U.S. anti-money-laundering
obligations relevant to Atlas Bank staff. It contains no customer information.

## Core obligations
- **Customer Identification Program (CIP):** verify the identity of each customer
  at account opening.
- **Customer Due Diligence (CDD):** understand the nature and purpose of customer
  relationships and develop a risk profile.
- **Enhanced Due Diligence (EDD):** apply heightened scrutiny to higher-risk
  customers, including those with complex ownership or exposure to high-risk
  jurisdictions.
- **Ongoing monitoring:** detect and report suspicious activity.

## Structuring
"Structuring" means breaking a large transaction into smaller amounts to evade the
$10,000 CTR reporting threshold. Structuring is itself a federal offense regardless
of whether the underlying funds are legitimate.

## Recordkeeping
Records supporting CTRs and SARs must be retained for **five years**.

# Northwind Trading LLC — AML Exception Register (Detail)

Detailed dispositions backing the Q2 exception summary. Compliance access only.

## EX-2026-0142 — Structuring
Between 2026-04-01 and 2026-04-08, eight cash deposits of $5,900–$6,100 were made
across three branches, aggregating **$47,900**. Each deposit fell below the
$10,000 CTR threshold. Pattern is consistent with **structuring** to evade
reporting. Recommended for SAR.

## EX-2026-0155 — Rapid movement of funds
An incoming wire of **$312,000** from a UAE counterparty was followed within 48
hours by outgoing wires to two Southeast Asian beneficiaries, leaving a near-zero
balance (pass-through). No documented commercial rationale. Recommended for SAR.

## EX-2026-0171 — High-risk jurisdiction counterparty
A **$88,500** wire involved a counterparty flagged for high-risk-jurisdiction
exposure. Disposition pending sanctions screening; see the restricted OFAC
screening note.

## EX-2026-0188 — Round-dollar wires
Four round-dollar wires totaling **$9,800**, inconsistent with the stated import
profile. Monitoring; not currently recommended for SAR.

# AML Monitoring — Quarterly Desk Metrics (2026 Q2)

Aggregate, de-identified monitoring statistics for the commercial portfolio. No
single-customer case detail appears in this note (analyst clearance).

| Metric | 2026 Q1 | 2026 Q2 |
|---|---|---|
| Alerts generated | 1,284 | 1,412 |
| Alerts escalated to compliance | 96 | 121 |
| Accounts under enhanced monitoring | 38 | 41 |
| Median alert disposition time (days) | 6.2 | 5.4 |

## Commentary
Escalations rose 26% quarter-over-quarter, driven primarily by cash-structuring
typologies in the cash-intensive trade segment. High-risk accounts continue to
account for a disproportionate share of escalations. Specific account
identifiers and case narratives are restricted to compliance reviewers.

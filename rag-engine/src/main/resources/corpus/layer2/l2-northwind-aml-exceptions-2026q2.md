# Northwind Trading LLC — Open AML Exceptions (2026 Q2)

Account: **NW-4471-08** · Risk rating: **High** · Reviewer: Compliance Desk

This summary lists the **open** AML exceptions for the Northwind account for the
current quarter. Three of the four open exceptions individually or in aggregate
exceed the **$5,000 SAR consideration threshold**; EX-2026-0142 also shows a
**structuring** pattern below the $10,000 CTR threshold.

|---|---|---|---|---|
| EX-2026-0142 | 2026-04-09 | Structuring (sub-$10k cash deposits) | 47,900 | Open |
| EX-2026-0155 | 2026-04-22 | Rapid movement of funds / pass-through | 312,000 | Open |
| EX-2026-0171 | 2026-05-11 | Counterparty in high-risk jurisdiction | 88,500 | Open |

## Assessment
EX-2026-0142 (structuring) and EX-2026-0155 (pass-through) **breach the reporting
threshold** and warrant a Suspicious Activity Report. Recommend preparing a draft
SAR covering EX-2026-0142 and EX-2026-0155 for BSA Officer review. EX-2026-0171
remains under review pending counterparty screening (see restricted OFAC note).

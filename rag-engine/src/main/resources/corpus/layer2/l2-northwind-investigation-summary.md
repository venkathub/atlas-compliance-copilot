investigation was opened into Northwind Trading LLC. Working hypothesis: the
account is being used to layer proceeds from an unidentified predicate offense,
using cash structuring (EX-2026-0142) to place funds and rapid cross-border wires
(EX-2026-0155) to move them.

## Recommendation

sanctions adjudication and source-of-wealth review. This summary, the SAR, and
all supporting material are restricted and confidential.
